A bit about me...

Developer at heart

I'm a 35 year old senior manager at Ph.Creative from Liverpool.

Since April 2014, I've been responsible for the web development team at Ph.Creative.
I started my career at Ph. in April 2008 as a Frontend Developer before becoming Senior Frontend Developer 4 years later.

Before that, I spent 5 years at Rapid Technologies as their sole web designer and developer responsible for maintaining their company website.

My experience spans multiple disciplines having worked closely with Backend Developers, Designer, UX/UI Designers, Project Managers even Finance but my main passion is HTML and CSS, delivering websites and leading a great team.

Home life

When I'm not living the fast pace of agency life I spend all my time with my other half and Son. Rachel and I have been together for 16 years, married in 2011 and had our first child James in 2012 with another one on the way!

We've spent all our lives in Liverpool both growing up in Norris Green before we bought our previous home in Anfield 11 years ago. Our adventures take us to Crosby next with a completion on our second home just around the corner.
